Vegetarian Rice Butter "Chicken"

A comforting, rich, and mildly spiced dish inspired by the classic Indian butter chicken—made vegetarian. This version swaps chicken for paneer, tofu, or plant-based chicken, simmered in a luscious tomato-butter-cream sauce, served over fragrant basmati rice. Perfect for a satisfying dinner.

Ingredients

  • 200g basmati rice
  • 250g paneer, tofu, or plant-based chicken chunks
  • 2 tbsp butter (or vegan butter)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp chili powder (adjust to taste)
